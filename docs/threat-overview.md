## Context
Digital scams are a common entry point for cyber incidents. They often combine social engineering with technical weaknesses in web applications and online services.

From a SOC perspective, understanding these threats is essential for effective detection, response, and prevention.

## Common Threats
- Phishing and malicious links  
- Fake websites impersonating legitimate services  
- Malicious advertisements  
- Malware delivery through downloads or scripts  

## Impact
- Credential theft  
- Financial loss  
- Data exposure  
- Malware infection  

## SOC Perspective
From a SOC (Blue Team) standpoint, these threats can be identified through log analysis, abnormal access patterns, user reports, and indicators of compromise.
